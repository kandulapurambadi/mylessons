Exercise 1: Fill in the blanks

1. Santhosh was very ______ to visit his grandparents' village.

2. The scarecrows were arranged to ______ away the birds.

3. The mango tree was heavily ______ with fruit.

4. The cuckoo's song was perhaps the most ______.

5. Santhosh ______ forward to his next visit.

Exercise 2: True or False

1. Santhosh traveled to the village by train. (False)

2. The village had lots of traffic. (False)

3. Pallavi showed Santhosh how to play with a tyre and stick. (True)

4. Santhosh knew how to swim. (False)

5. Santhosh watched television during his village stay. (False)

2. What did Santhosh see in the paddy fields during the journey?

4. What did Santhosh realize about his stay in the village?
