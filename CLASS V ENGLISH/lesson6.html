The Wise Judgement

"Surely the sound of money is a fair payment for the smell of meatballs."
- Nasruddin, the Scholar

Characters in the Story

Nasruddin (The Scholar)

Telugu: నస్రుద్దీన్ (పండితుడు)

The wise magistrate who delivers a fair judgement. He uses clever thinking to solve the dispute between the traveller and the eating-house keeper.

The Poor Traveller

Telugu: పేద ప్రయాణీకుడు

A hungry man with no money who only smells the meatballs. He is honest about his poverty and asks for mercy.

Eating-house Keeper

Telugu: భోజనశాల యజమాని

The owner of the eating-house who gets angry when the traveller smells his food without paying. He demands payment for the smell.
